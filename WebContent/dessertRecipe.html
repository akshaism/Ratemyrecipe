Cranberry Molasses Cake with Sweet Butter Sauce





Author: Blake Cleary

Vote Sum = 2

Number of votes = 4

Description: This classic cranberry molasses cake, served warm with sweet butter sauce and a scoop of vanilla ice cream, is an extra-special treat for a holiday or really any night of the week.

Recipe Steps:

step 1 - Heat oven to 350Â°F. Spray bottom and sides of 13x9-inch pan with cooking spray.

step 2 - In large bowl, beat eggs, 3/4 cup sugar, the molasses and water with whisk. In medium bowl, mix flour, salt and baking soda. Stir flour mixture into liquid mixture just until moistened; fold in cranberries. Pour mixture in pan.

step 3 - Bake 37 to 42 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 20 minutes.

step 4 - In 2-quart saucepan, heat Sweet Butter Sauce ingredients to simmering over medium heat, stirring constantly, until slightly thickened and sugar is dissolved.

step 5 - Spray edge of sharp knife with cooking spray; cut warm cake into 5 rows by 4 rows. Serve cake with warm butter sauce.
